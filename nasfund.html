largest private-sector superannuation

nasfund is the largest private-sector superannuation fund in Papua New Guinea with net assets of over K5 billion. Evolving attitudes toward personal finance, greater consumer expectations and a more crowded marketplace meant the fund's brand needed updating to mirror a growing organisation that appreciates and is concerned for the future of its members.

providing financial freedom to its members

Our objective was to design a fresh brand identity that aligned with nasfund's core ethos of providing financial freedom to its members. It was vital to highlight the fund's goal of improving members' well-being through a range of superannuation options.

pointing to the spirit and origins of South Pacific currency

We began by developing an updated feel to the existing brand logo, progressing on the fund's legacy by creating a simplified version of the kina shell, pointing to the spirit and origins of South Pacific currency, whilst presenting a contemporary cleanness. We devised comprehensive brand guidelines, a new website, restyled all internal documents and restructured external products. RESULT

Gaining a better understanding of personal savings is essential to everybody's outlook and lifestyle, so we're glad to have aided nasfund with its aim of ensuring its members are 'ready for tomorrow'.
