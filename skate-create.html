SKATE CAMP

Skate Camp is a camp themed area in where we hold The training for skateboarding. This skate park will be used for skate classes for all different age groups of children. Run by a professional skate school, these teachers Can hold up to 4 classes per day running for 1 hour each. The teachers themselves will be teaching kids from the age of 5 years old up to 17 years old, how to balance on a board, how to kick and push and most of all try to do a trick.

THE STUDIO

The Studio is a stand along structure that houses 1 or 2 artists over the selected period of time. The structure is a custom made structure that is made of white walls. Artists over the period of time of the activation are to change the custom made structure into a piece of art. Whilst the artist is housed there for the period, the artist holds daily artist workshops to the Westfield customers.
