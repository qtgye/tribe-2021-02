SKATE CAMP

Skate Camp is a camp themed area in where we hold The training for skateboarding. This skate park will be used for skate classes for all different age groups of children. Run by a professional skate school, these teachers Can hold up to 4 classes per day running for 1 hour each. The teachers themselves will be teaching kids from the age of 5 years old up to 17 years old, how to balance on a board, how to kick and push and most of all try to do a trick. Its with these skills the kids will be empowered to go outside and have some fun with their friends. Skate and Create want to bring something more that just a shopping experience to its visitors, but something that can grow and develop its visitors, which in turn places Westfield top of mind when visiting their next shopping centre.

teaching local consumers the difference between fresh milk and UHT milk.

PNG consumers are not used to having readily available, affordable and locally produced fresh milk. For the first time Ilimo dairy can provide local fresh milk at an affordable retail price, the benefits of which are obvious to expatriates from dairy producing countries, however demand must be generated through education and information to the less aware local market segments. The greatest challenge for our team was teaching local consumers the difference between fresh milk and UHT milk.
